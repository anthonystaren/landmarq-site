FAQ

Common questions.

How quickly can I get started?
Most users are fully configured and live within 10 minutes. The onboarding flow walks you through selecting your markets, activating agents, and setting your first intelligence watches.
Does LandMarq integrate with our existing tools?
Pro and Enterprise plans include native integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, and email. Enterprise clients can connect to proprietary systems via our API.
What data sources do the agents use?
Agents pull from CoStar, Kastle Systems, public permit portals, corporate filing databases, job posting APIs, news feeds, and macro economic data sources. Enterprise clients can connect proprietary data.
Is my firm's data kept confidential?
Yes. Your data is never used to train our models or shared with other clients. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Enterprise plans include dedicated infrastructure options.
Can we build a white-label version for our firm?
Enterprise plans include full white-label capability — your brand, your domain, your client experience. Contact us to discuss custom development of firm-specific agents.
What happens after the 14-day trial?
You'll be prompted to select a plan. Your settings, watches, and intelligence history are preserved. If you don't upgrade, access is paused — your data is retained for 30 days.
