"The best brokers in every market weren't smarter — they were better informed, earlier. We built LandMarq to give every professional that edge, automatically."

Commercial real estate has always been an information game. The broker who hears about a tenant displacement first, who knows about a zoning change before it's public, who has a warm introduction ready when a company starts hiring aggressively — that broker wins the deal.

For decades, that edge came from relationships, tenure, and luck. We believe it should come from intelligence — continuous, curated, and delivered to you before the competition even knows to look.

LandMarq was built by a team of CRE veterans and AI engineers who spent years watching talented brokers waste hours on research that should have taken minutes. Six AI agents. 25+ live data sources — from FRED and Treasury yields to prediction markets and SEC filings. One executive dashboard. Every market you cover, 24 hours a day.

Our Principles

What we believe.

⚡
Action over dashboards
Data that sits in a report doesn't win deals. LandMarq agents perform work — they don't just display it. Every insight comes with a clear next action.
🎯
First-mover or nothing
In CRE, timing is the product. We optimize every agent, every alert, and every delivery to put you in the room first — not second.
🔒
Your data stays yours
We never use your deal data to train models or share with other clients. What happens on your dashboard stays on your dashboard.
🛠️
Built for practitioners
Every feature was designed with active brokers and managing directors — not theorists. If it doesn't help close deals, it doesn't ship.
📡
Always on, never missed
Markets don't wait for office hours. Our agents run 24/7 and surface what matters — from overnight permit filings to Sunday morning news.
🗣️
Plain language first
Add intelligence in plain English. "Track tech tenants expanding in the 310" — not a dropdown menu. Natural language is the interface.
From the Founder

A letter from our founder.

I started my career in management consulting at Ernst & Young, then spent over a decade in B2B data analytics and technology business development. Along the way I built AppFirms, a software development firm, and learned what it takes to ship products that actually move the needle for clients. Today I work as an M&A advisor and investor — and I kept seeing the same gap everywhere I looked.

Commercial real estate was the widest gap of all. Brokers managing $200M+ pipelines were still toggling between CoStar tabs, permit portal PDFs, and spreadsheet trackers. The intelligence existed — it just wasn't connected, curated, or actionable.

LandMarq is my answer to that problem. Six AI agents that never sleep, monitoring every signal across your markets and delivering what matters to a single dashboard every morning. No more missed leads. No more surprise vacancies. No more LOI clauses slipping through.

— The Founder
